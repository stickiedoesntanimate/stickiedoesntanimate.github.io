Welcome to the Balatro Joker Generator!
Press the Generate button to generate a Joker! Click a mod on the left to enable things from that mod (such as Tarot cards, Spectral cards and Planet cards.)
Most things generated are hyperlinked, click on it to open its wiki page! Go to the settings tab to change various settings, such as the background.
